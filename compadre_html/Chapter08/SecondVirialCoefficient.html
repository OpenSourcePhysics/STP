Program SecondVirialCoefficient

Approximation techniques are used to study the effect of inter-particle interactions in gases. A common technique is the virial density expansion. The first-order expansion for the ideal gas is given by $$\frac{PV}{NkT} = 1 + \rho B_2(T),$$ where the second virial coefficient is $$B_2(T) = 2\pi \int_0^{\infty} [1 - e^{u(r)/kT}] r^2dr$$ for a spherically symmetric inter-particle potential $u(r)$.

Program SecondVirialCoefficient computes $B_2(T)$ for the Lennard-Jones potential.
